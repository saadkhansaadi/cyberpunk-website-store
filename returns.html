Return Policies

The 14-Day Guarantee

If your physical gear doesn't meet your expectations or the fit is incorrect, you have 14 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return protocol.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Items must be unworn and in original condition.
  • Original tags and packaging must be intact.
  • Digital art and custom commissions are non-refundable.

Initiating a Return

To start a return, please visit your User Profile or contact our support terminal. You will be provided with a return shipping label and instructions for your sector.

Once we receive and verify your item, your credits will be refunded to your original payment method within 5-7 solar days.
