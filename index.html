Frequently Asked Queries

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer global shipping. Orders above $50 automatically qualify for free priority delivery to anywhere in the world.

How long do custom commissions take?

Digital portraits typically take 3-5 business days. Once approved, physical printing takes an additional 2 days.

Are the limited edition drops restocked?

No. To maintain exclusivity, once a limited edition collection sells out, those specific designs are vaulted permanently.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 14-day return window for unworn apparel. Custom commissions and digital art are non-refundable.
